Understanding Traffic Congestion

Traffic congestion is a major issue affecting urban mobility. Investigating its causes and solutions is essential for better transportation planning.

Causes of Traffic Congestion

  • High vehicle volume
  • Insufficient road capacity
  • Traffic accidents
  • Poorly timed traffic signals

Solutions to Reduce Congestion

  • Better public transport
  • Smart traffic management
  • Road expansion
  • Encouraging carpooling
